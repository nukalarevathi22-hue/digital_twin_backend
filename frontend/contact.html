Frequently Asked Questions

❓ How does the digital twin technology work?
Our digital twin technology creates a personalized 3D model of your heart using medical imaging data. This virtual replica is then synchronized with real-time health data from wearables and monitoring devices to provide continuous cardiac insights.
❓ Is my health data secure?
Yes, we use enterprise-grade encryption and comply with HIPAA regulations to ensure all patient data remains secure and confidential. Your privacy is our top priority.
❓ Can I use VirtuHeart with my existing medical devices?
VirtuHeart integrates with most popular wearables and medical devices including Apple Watch, Fitbit, blood pressure monitors, and smart scales. Contact us for specific device compatibility.
❓ How do I get started as a healthcare provider?
Healthcare providers can schedule a demo with our team to see the platform in action. We'll guide you through setup, integration with your EHR system, and training for your staff.

Cardiac Emergency?

If you or someone you know is experiencing chest pain, shortness of breath, or other symptoms of a heart attack, please seek immediate medical attention.
